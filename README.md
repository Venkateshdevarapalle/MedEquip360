# 🏥 MedEquip360 AI

### AI-Powered Medical Equipment Supply & Service Management Platform

MedEquip360 AI is a full-stack healthcare technology platform designed to streamline the management of medical equipment, hospital operations, supplier services, warranties, maintenance schedules, and service requests through a centralized intelligent dashboard.

The platform combines modern SaaS design, healthcare workflows, analytics, and AI-driven insights to improve equipment lifecycle management and operational efficiency for hospitals and medical equipment suppliers.

# 📌 Project Overview

Healthcare organizations often struggle with fragmented systems for inventory management, equipment servicing, warranty monitoring, maintenance scheduling, and supplier coordination.

MedEquip360 AI provides a unified solution that enables hospitals and suppliers to manage the complete lifecycle of medical equipment through a single platform.
